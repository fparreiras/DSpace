Jesse Owens

The Jesse Owens Photographs collection is a sample of the photographs held at the OSU Archives. The gallery contains photographs from Owens' early track years, OSU activities, 1936 Berlin Olympics, and post-Olympic activities.

Owens is featured in an exhibition in the Thompson Library Gallery from January 9 through May 5, 2013.
