Celebrating the 10th Anniversary of the Knowledge Bank

The Knowledge Bank, a service of The Ohio State University Libraries, provides persistent and reliable access to the intellectual output of The Ohio State University. Faculty, staff, and students are invited to deposit their research and scholarship. Colleges, Departments, and Centers can distribute their working papers, technical reports, conference proceedings, and other research material via the Knowledge Bank. For assistance in depositing documents or for more information please contact us.
