Open Access Fund for OSU Authors

The Open Access Fund for OSU Authors is a pilot project jointly funded by University Libraries and the Health Sciences Library. The fund supports Open Access article processing charges for OSU faculty, staff, and enrolled students publishing peer-reviewed articles in eligible Open Access journals.
